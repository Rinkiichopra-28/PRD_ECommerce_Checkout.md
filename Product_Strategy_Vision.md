## 1. Vision & Opportunity
* **North Star Metric:** Completed Monthly Orders per Active User.
* **Problem Opportunity:** Cart abandonment accounts for $18B in lost industry revenue annually.
* **Target Outcome:** Recapture 15% of lost revenues by eliminating checkout friction.

## 3. Prioritization Framework (RICE)
* **Reach:** 100,000 monthly active users entering checkout.
* **Impact:** High (3/3) — Direct revenue driver.
* **Confidence:** 80% — Backed by analytics and exit survey data.
* **Effort:** 2 Person-Months.
* **RICE Score:** `(100,000 * 3 * 0.80) / 2` = **120,000**
